Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

A focused set of tools to make sure every coupon gets used.

📷

Instant OCR Scanning

Point your camera at any coupon or import from your photo library. On-device AI extracts the company, code, discount, and expiry automatically.

🔔

Expiry Reminders

Color-coded urgency chips show which coupons are expiring soon. Optional push notifications alert you days before a deal disappears.

🔍

Search, Sort & Filter

Find any coupon instantly. Sort by company, expiry, date added, or highest discount. Filter by category to zero in on what you need.

🔒

100% Offline & Private

All data stays on your device. No accounts, no servers, no analytics, no ads. Your coupons are nobody's business but yours.

⭐

Favorites & Archive

Pin your go-to coupons for quick access. Used and expired coupons auto-archive so your wallet stays clean.

📦

Export Your Data

Export all your coupons as JSON anytime. Your data belongs to you, and you can take it wherever you want.

Three steps to never miss a deal

From paper coupon to organized wallet in seconds.

1

Scan or snap

Use your camera to scan a physical coupon, or import an image from your photo library. You can also add coupons manually.

2

Review and save

Folio's on-device OCR extracts the details automatically. Review, tweak if needed, add categories and notes, then save.

3

Use before you lose

Browse your organized wallet, get notified before expiry, and tap to copy the code when you're ready to redeem at checkout.
